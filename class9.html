Teaching Methodology

How each chapter is taught

A proven 4-phase methodology that takes students from zero to confident in every chapter.

Phase 1

Conceptual Foundation

Every topic starts with "why" before "how" — building genuine understanding from real-world phenomena.

Phase 2

Derivations & Theory

Key formulas are derived logically — never memorized. Students understand where equations come from.

Phase 3

Guided Numericals

Worked examples with full step-by-step explanations, building towards independent problem-solving.

Phase 4

Practice & Assessment

Board-style practice questions, regular tests, and personalized feedback to ensure mastery.
